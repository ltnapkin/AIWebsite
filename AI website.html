What Are Robots?

Robots are machines designed to perform tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. They can be used in various industries, from manufacturing to healthcare, and they come in many shapes and sizes!

Cool Robots

  • ASIMO: Honda's humanoid robot that can walk, run, and climb stairs.
  • Spot: Boston Dynamics' robotic dog known for its agility and versatility.
  • Roomba: An intelligent vacuum cleaner that navigates and cleans your home.

The Future of Robotics

The future of robotics is bright! With advancements in AI, robots are becoming smarter, more efficient, and more integrated into our daily lives. From personal assistants to autonomous vehicles, the possibilities are endless!
